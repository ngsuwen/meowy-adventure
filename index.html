Your cards in game are composed of draw pile, current hand and discard pile.
Next
After playing a card from your hand, the card will be placed in the discard pile, and the hand will be replaced with a new card from the draw pile. When the player tries to draw from an empty deck, the discard pile will be shuffled back into the draw pile.
Next
Playing a card will cost one point of Mana. When the mana reaches 0, player will have to end the turn.
